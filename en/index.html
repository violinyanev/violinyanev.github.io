Sofia

When in Sofia

Some useful tips

  • If you arrive at Terminal 2, you can take the metro straight to the city center.
  • If you arrive at Terminal 1, you can take a shuttle bus to Terminal 2 for free, and then get on the metro.
  • On the metro, you can use your bank card's contactless function to go through the barriers, you will be charged a fixed price (1,6 BGN/0,8 EUR).
  • You can always get a taxi, but beware of the prices. The price per km should not be higher than 1,3 BGN.
  • It's better to use ATMs from bank offices. There are some random ATMs in the city which charge extra fees and may block your card.

Day trips from Sofia

  • Koprivshtitsa - one of the most charming little towns in Bulgaria with traditional colourful houses and cobbled streets
  • Rila Monastery - the largest and most famous Eastern Orthodox monastery in Bulgaria
  • Seven Rila lakes - picturesque set of glacial lakes in the Rila mountain, great for a one-day hike
  • Vitosha mountain - there are a lot of hiking trails, our favourite is the path to the Golden Bridges
  • Old town of Plovdiv - the second largest city in Bulgaria has a beautiful old town which combines the culture and architecture from Antiquity, Middle Ages and the Bulgarian Renaissance
